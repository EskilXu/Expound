Theoretical Foundation
The Four-Layer Cognitive Architecture
FLCA is our proprietary diagnostic framework. It explains why AI hallucinates — and what you can actually do about it.
Layer 1
World Model
Grounding in physical and environmental reality
Layer 2
Reasoning
Abstract and causal inference capabilities
Layer 3
Metacognition
Self-monitoring and confidence calibration
Layer 4
Motivation
Internal drive for accuracy and alignment
🔍

Core Insight: The Care Gap. Current LLMs have the surface of Layer 2 but lack Layers 1, 3, and 4. These layers have generative dependency — you cannot skip a layer. Hallucination is not a fixable bug; it is a symptom of architectural absence. This diagnosis changes how you design, deploy, and hedge AI systems.
